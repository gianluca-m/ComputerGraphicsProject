Proposal


For our Image, we want to show the beauty of space with an Astronaut that the world has never seen before. There will be some objects floating around in space that do not belong there.



As things like Asteroids are typically floating around in Space, we plan to add something like this to our image. And this is a perfect entry point to develop some procedurally generated objects.
We also plan to add planets in the background for which we can use the bump map texture and mip-mapping since they will be far away from the camera.


Of course the image would not be complete without an actual Astronaut. To fit the theme of the course, we wanted to put something completely random into our scene: For example a pirate ship or some cool looking animal.

Additional Ideas:

  • Space Nebula
  • Clouds around the planet earth surface
  • Stars in background
