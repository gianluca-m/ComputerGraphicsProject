Proposal

Source: pixabay

For our Image, we want to show the beauty of space with an astronaut and/or space ship that the world has never seen before. To fit the theme of the project "Out of Place", we want to put something completely random into our scene: For example a pirate ship, or a cool looking animal as an astronaut.

Source: pixabay

As things like asteroids are typically floating around in space, we plan to add something like this to our image. And this is a perfect entry point to develop some procedurally generated objects.

We also want to add planets and moons in the background for which we can use the bump map texture and mip-mapping since they will be far away from the camera.

To utilize the Participating Media feature, we plan on adding clouds around the planet earth surface. Another idea could be to have floating clouds somewhere in the scene, where the space ship is "floating in". Or, maybe one of the planets could just be a sphere of smoke.

Additional Ideas:

  • Space Nebula
  • Stars in background

If we see that trying to add all the features into a single final scene is not possible, because it will overload the scene and look bad, we will just render certain features in seperate scenes to validate them.
